I first got my intrest in graphic design watching my cousin work in applications like PhotoShop and Illustrator. Watching him mix colors, and create these beautiful pieces of work was inspiring to me. So I decided to install the software on my dad's computer. From my perspective it was like a baby working the controls of a spaceship. When I started middle school, my skills sharpened in a technology class i was taking. I learned PhotoShop and what each tool does,

I made a CD cover from scratch, I've mixed music, and I've even made a short movie. I've earned an industry certification in Computing Fundimentals and have been working on my second certification, PhotoShop. My new goal is to become a proffesional graphic artist .
