Biography

I first got my intrest in graphic design watching my cousin work in applications like PhotoShop and Illustrator. Watching him mix colors, and create these beautiful pieces of work was inspiring to me. So I decided to install the software on my dad's computer. From my perspective it was like a baby working the controls of a spaceship. When I started middle school, my skills sharpened in a technology class I was taking. I learned how to use PhotoShop and the Adobe Suite effectively, I have earned an industry certification in Computing Fundimentals and have been working on my second certification in PhotoShop.
